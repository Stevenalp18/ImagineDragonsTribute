Timeline

  • 2008: Dan forms first group while attending Brigham Young University
  • 2009: Wayne Sermon is brought on board by Drummer, Andrew Beck
  • 2009: Sermon recruited Ben McKee
  • 2009: Moved to Las Vegas, Released a self-titled EP, Imagine Dragons
  • 2009: Called to fill for Train due to the lead singer being sick at the time
  • 2010: EP, Hell and Silence released
  • 2011: McKee brought Dan Platzman on board
  • 2012: Hear Me and Continued Silence released as an EP
  • 2012: It's Time, released and ranked 15th of Billboard top 100
  • 2012: Debut album, Night Visions, ranks 2 in Billboard top 200
  • 2012: Radioactive, ranked number 1 on the Billboard alternative siongs
  • 2013: Night Visions(Deluxe) and Soundtracks, Iron Man III, Infinity Blade, Hunger Games, etc.
  • 2014: Smoke and Mirrors, Transformers, and League of Legends World Championship Soundtrack
  • 2017: Evolve album created and Levitate for Passengers
  • 2017: Believer performed at Superbowl ad for the Nintendo Switch
  • 2018-2020: Other songs released and many soundtrack contracts obtained
  • 2020-present: Mercury Released, worldwide sensation, they are known everywhere
